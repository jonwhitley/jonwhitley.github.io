Barloween

Children's Book

Barloween is this goofball Halloween story that came to me one day in the form of one of the most head-slappingly unbearable puns I've ever come up with. It was a whole lot of fun to write and I can only hope it's just as much fun to read. Per the Kindle Direct Listing,

"A happy-go-lucky pumpkin named Barlow and a colorful cast of companions set out on an epic Halloween quest through a deep, dark, very very dark forest.

Featuring a fun-loving crew of characters and sprinkled with subtle humor, Barloween is a Halloween adventure story meant to bring fun to children of all ages, and anyone else besides."
