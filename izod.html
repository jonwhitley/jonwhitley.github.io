Izod the Christmas Dragon

Children's Book

Izod the Christmas Dragon is a children's book I wrote down for fun one day, and it just sat on my hard drive for several years, but every time I pulled it out it made me grin, so eventually I decided to just go ahead and publish it for everybody to enjoy. Per the Kindle Direct Listing,

"On a snowy yuletide night, high on the banks of the Wetherian Mountains, a sharp-eyed dragon named Izod sets out on a quest that will lead him across the snow-covered peaks of the Kingdom of Bellwether, in a journey of Christmas discovery on his way to becoming the legendary Christmas Dragon.

Featuring wry wit and a set of original illustrations, Izod the Christmas Dragon is the perfect Christmas story for readers of all ages."

Currently available on Apple Books, Kindle Direct Publishing, Barnes and Noble Press, and Rakuten Kobo. I hope you like it!
