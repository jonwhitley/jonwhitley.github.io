The Western Front

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Map

The Western Front is a map for Valve's Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. It is inspired by the trench warfare of World War 1. This map offers a variety of strategic options, with trenches, bunkers, ruins, ramps, and sandbags to use as cover for short or long-range fights.
