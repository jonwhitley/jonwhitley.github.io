Night on the Western Front

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Campaign

Night on the Western Front is a map for Valve's Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. It is inspired by the trench warfare of World War 1. Night on the Western Front is really just a second version of another CSGO map I made called The Western Front. They have the same layout, but this version takes place at night, during an active thunderstorm. I thought it made for a really cool atmosphere.
