Books

I have written and published two books (so far, both children's books). Both are lightly illustrated, and both are direct-published and available in either print or e-book form. I owe a debt of gratitude to Apple Books, Kindle Direct Publishing, Barnes and Noble, and Rakuten Kobo for providing me with the means of self-publication. Please, pick up a copy if you can, read them, love them, share them with your family. Thank you!

Left 4 Dead 2

I have spent more time than I care to admit playing and building campaigns for Left 4 Dead, and I take pride in the fact that while my campaigns might not be the smoothest or the most stable, they are all very distinctive in terms of visuals and experience. I really have put a ton of effort into them, and every one of these campaigns took several months of relentless work to complete.
Every time I finish one I swear I'm never going to do it again, but, well, here we are 3 deep, and I will neither confirm nor deny that another campaign is in the works.
